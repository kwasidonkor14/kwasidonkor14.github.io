Why We Love Watching Video Games More Than Playing Them

Why We Love Watching Video Games More Than Playing Them explores how gaming transformed from a solo pastime to a shared global experience. Kwasi Donkor dives into the rise of streaming culture from Twitch chats to YouTube highlights showing why millions find as much joy in watching as in playing. Blending entertainment, community, and connection, it’s a look at how gaming became the ultimate spectator sport for a digital generation.
